Community FAQ

Where should I start if I feel stuck?

Start with the core loop checklist: steady berry output, consistent feeding, then ROI-focused upgrades. Follow the Beginner Guide and pick 2–3 upgrades to prioritize.

Is WebGL progress saved?

WebGL saves depend on browser storage. If you clear site data or use private browsing, saves may reset. For stable long-term saves, the Steam version is usually more consistent.

How do I reduce lag and audio issues?

Try fullscreen after the game loads, close heavy tabs, and avoid battery saver modes. See: WebGL performance.

Can I share screenshots and fan content?

Yes—share your screenshots, clips, and guides. If you use third-party assets, make sure you have permission.
