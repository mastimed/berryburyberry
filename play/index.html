Quick tips

  • Upgrade often — short loops beat long grinding.
  • Early game: prioritize upgrades that increase berry generation/collection speed.
  • When the next upgrade feels slow, buy one “efficiency” upgrade instead of forcing it.

Troubleshooting WebGL

  • Lag/stutter: close heavy tabs, disable extensions, try Chrome/Edge.
  • No sound: click inside the page once (browsers block audio until user interaction).
  • Black screen: refresh, then click “Load the game”.
